¡A couple of Questions!

If you've asked yourself a lot of questions about what Spaceland is, we can answer a couple of them here. Get ready!

¿What is Spaceland?

Spaceland is a server where you will find games and fun, its main inspiration and fun engine are memes, in addition to that it is a place where your fun has no end and you can have a good time with your friends.

¿Is this real or are we in Space?

This is very real, we have a fantastic community of support, we have ongoing projects that advance every day, this is not an illusion, it is real, join us!

¿Does Spaceland have agreements with other Projects?

Yes! And it really is a lot of fun, we have an integration for the distribution of DATZ in a big way, it really is a lot of fun. Can you imagine winning the coin while having fun? Now it is possible thanks to us!

¿Do you have a Future Project Vision?

The truth is that yes, the futuristic vision is to become one of the largest and most enjoyable servers. Being a promoter server that everyone can turn to if they need help. This is really fun!

¿Who started all This?

The Spaceland project started as just a dream, which a few years later IdolsTheLunar (CEO of Spaceland), seeing the opportunity to create a project that would bear fruit, revived all hope and decided to take this great project forward.

¿Who do I contact if I want to contribute to the project?

You can contact the CEO of Spaceland (IdolsTheLunar) and make your proposal, any suggestion will be taken into account, there are no bad ideas, so this server accepts suggestions.

¿Who do I contact if I have Questions?

You can come to the frequently asked questions section, and answer each of your questions Remember we are here to help us!
