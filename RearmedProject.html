Re Armed, is a project of NFTs created under the Atomic Hub market, it is about monsters with different rarities, powers and elements. Clearly this project is inspired by Mosters. What do you say about collecting them all?

These types of collectibles can be obtained or simply found in the Atomic Hub market, they can be bought and collected in your WAX wallet. It should be noted that you can get them for free on our Discord server and participating in all seasonal Events.
